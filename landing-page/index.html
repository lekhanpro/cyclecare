Features

Everything You Need.
Nothing You Don't.

Powerful cycle tracking designed around your privacy and wellbeing.

📅

Smart Calendar

Interactive calendar with color-coded period, fertile, and ovulation days. Navigate months and tap to log.

🧠

AI Predictions

Machine learning predictions that adapt to your unique cycle patterns with confidence scoring.

🌸

Fertility Tracking

Ovulation dates, fertile windows, BBT charting, and cervical observation tracking.

📝

Daily Logging

Track 40+ symptoms, moods, flow, temperature, weight, water, sleep, intimacy, and tests.

📈

Insights Dashboard

Cycle analytics with trend charts, symptom heatmaps, mood patterns, and regularity scores.

💊

Birth Control

Pill reminders with streaks, IUD tracking, injection schedules, and medication management.

🔔

Smart Reminders

Period, pill, and hydration notifications with quiet hours and customizable channels.

📥

Data Export

Export your complete health data in CSV or JSON. Full backup and restore support.

🔒

Biometric Lock

PIN and fingerprint/face authentication. Your most sensitive data, fully protected.
