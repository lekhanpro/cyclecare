# Pregnancy Week by Week Guide

## First Trimester (Weeks 1-12)
- Week 4: Implantation occurs
- Week 6: Heartbeat detectable
- Week 8: All major organs forming
- Week 12: Risk of miscarriage decreases significantly

## Second Trimester (Weeks 13-26)
- Week 16: Gender may be visible on ultrasound
- Week 20: Halfway point - movement felt (quickening)
- Week 24: Viability milestone

## Third Trimester (Weeks 27-40)
- Week 28: Eyes can open
- Week 32: Bones hardening
- Week 36: Baby may engage in pelvis
- Week 40: Full term

## Prenatal Care Schedule
- Monthly visits until week 28
- Bi-weekly visits from weeks 28-36
- Weekly visits from week 36 until delivery
